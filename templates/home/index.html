About

Agronation associate as a nation of farmers who works for betterment of farmers. Todays farmer faces a lot of problems like:-
1)Unavailability of prescription
2)Lack of mechanization
3)Inadequate supply chain
Our aim is to solve these problem by implementing real time analysis of the farmland data and geographical factors to provide scientific prescription, inclusion of IoT and farmer to enterprise (F2E) business platform
